Ternary plot makerRC Explanation About

Being frustrated with existing options for creating ternary plots, I thought a better, quicker option should be available. After finding Daven Quinn's d3-ternary library, I decided to hack together a small webpage for quickly making ternary plots for some of my courses. Afterwards I decided to develop it into a more user-friendly online tool available to anyone for free.

Thanks to Daven Quinn for developing the d3-ternary library. SVG download option based on SVG Crowbar. Explanation slides made with reveal.js and reveal-d3. The tables are HandsOnTables, which are infinitely better than textarea for input!

Please note that this project is always under development. If you have found any bugs/typos or have suggestions or feature requests than I would like to hear about it. Please send an e-mail to julesblom@gmail.com.

This site works best in the newest browsers! Tested in Chrome & Firefox

What is a ternary plot?

A ternary plot is useful chart for vizualing three variables which sum to a constant. This constant is usually 1.0 or 100%.

Let's add some labels to our axes! Suppose we have a soil sample that consists of three soil types: sand, silt, and clay. These three together form 100% of our soil sample.

We have three axes, one for each component. In each 'corner' of the triangle we're at 100% of one of the elements.
